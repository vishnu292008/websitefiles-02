About us

Founded by Nishil and Vignesh, we're young entrepreneurs who understand the unique challenges gaming café owners face in today's fast-paced digital world. As avid gamers ourselves, we saw how local gaming cafés struggled to keep up with customer service demands while trying to create the perfect gaming environment. That's why we created GameVoice – an AI-powered solution that brings cutting-edge voice and chat support to gaming cafés across the country. Our intelligent virtual assistants handle everything from basic inquiries to complex customer support and being able to book game stations, allowing café owners to focus on what really matters: creating amazing gaming experiences. We combine our passion for gaming culture with innovative AI. Whether it's answering questions about game availability, managing bookings, or providing technical support, our AI solutions work tirelessly to keep your customers happy and your business running smoothly.
